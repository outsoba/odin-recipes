Vegan Meat Lasagna

This is the best Vegan Lasagna recipe in the world.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Tomate Sauce
  • Vegan Minced Meat
  • Vegan Cheese
  • Bechamel Sauce

Steps

  1. make meat sauce
  2. put lasagna sheets, sauces and cheese in casserole dish
  3. put in oven
